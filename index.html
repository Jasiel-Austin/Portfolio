Data Cleaning using SQL

In this project, I conducted a data cleaning on the raw data of company layoffs around the world in recent years. This was done by removing data duplicates, standardizing inputs, removing null/blank values and deleting any necessary columns and rows based on intended database use. As a result, the data was ready for visualization.

Data Visualization of Survey Results in PowerBI

In this project, I analyzed a survey of data professionals, utilizing Power Query within Power BI to clean and transform the raw data. I then created insightful visualizations and finalized a comprehensive dashboard. The primary objective was to identify and present the average salary across different roles, locations, and preferred programming languages within the survey pool.
